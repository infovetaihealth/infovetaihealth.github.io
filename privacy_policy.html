Privacy Policy – VetAIHealth

Last updated: February 2026

The VetAIHealth application (“the App”) respects and protects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use the App.

1. About the Application

VetAIHealth is an AI-based informational tool designed to provide preliminary, non-diagnostic insights related to animal health. The App does not provide medical or veterinary diagnoses and does not replace professional veterinary care.

2. Information We Collect

The App may temporarily process the following data voluntarily provided by the user:

  • Photos of pets
  • Text descriptions of symptoms or health-related concerns

Additionally, limited technical data (such as IP address, device type, and system logs) may be processed automatically to ensure proper operation, security, and performance of the App.

3. How We Use the Data

Collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Generate an AI-based informational assessment requested by the user
  • Maintain functionality, security, and performance of the App

Data is not used for profiling, marketing, or advertising purposes.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Uploaded images and text are stored only for the duration of the analysis. Once processing is completed, all user-submitted content is automatically deleted and is not retained on our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services to support its functionality:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) – used to display rewarded advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID), IP address, and ad interaction data. Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on user consent.

For more information on how Google processes personal data, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. User Consent (GDPR)

Where required by applicable law, users are asked to provide consent for personalized advertising. Users may choose non-personalized ads. Consent preferences are stored locally on the device and can be changed by clearing the App’s data or adjusting device settings.

7. Data Security

All communications with the App’s servers are secured using HTTPS encryption. Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

⚠️ AI Disclaimer:
VetAIHealth provides AI-generated informational assessments based on automated analysis and may contain inaccuracies, limitations, or errors.

Medical & Veterinary Disclaimer:
The information provided by the App does not constitute medical or veterinary advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a licensed veterinarian for professional evaluation, diagnosis, and appropriate care.

8. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to information about your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for personalized advertising

Since user-submitted data is not permanently stored, deletion occurs automatically after processing is completed.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be published on this page along with the updated revision date.
