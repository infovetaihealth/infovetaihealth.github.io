Privacy Policy – VetAIHealth

Last updated: February 2026

The VetAIHealth application (“the App”) respects and protects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use the App.

1. About the Application

VetAIHealth is an AI-based informational tool designed to provide preliminary assessments related to animal health. The App does not provide medical or veterinary diagnoses and does not replace professional veterinary care.

2. Information We Collect

The App may temporarily process the following data provided voluntarily by the user:

  • Photos of your pet
  • Text descriptions of symptoms or health-related concerns

Additionally, limited technical data (such as IP address, device type, or system logs) may be processed automatically to ensure proper operation and security.

3. How We Use the Data

The collected data is used exclusively to generate an AI-based informational assessment requested by the user and to maintain the functionality and security of the App.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Uploaded images and text are stored only for the duration of the analysis. After processing is completed, all user-submitted data is automatically deleted and is not retained on our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services to support its functionality:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) – used to display rewarded advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID) and ad interaction data. Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on user consent.

For more information about how Google processes data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. User Consent (GDPR)

Where required by law, users are asked for consent regarding personalized advertising. Users may choose limited (non-personalized) ads. Consent preferences are stored locally on the device and can be changed by clearing the app’s data.

7. Data Security

All communications with the server are secured via HTTPS encryption. Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect data from unauthorized access.

⚠️ AI & Medical Disclaimer:
VetAIHealth provides AI-generated informational assessments which may contain inaccuracies. The results do not constitute a medical or veterinary diagnosis. Always consult a licensed veterinarian for professional advice and treatment.

8. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request information about your data, request deletion, or withdraw consent for personalized advertising.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be published on this page along with the updated revision date.
