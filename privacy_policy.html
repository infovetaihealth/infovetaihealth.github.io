Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025  ·  VetAIHealth

📷

Photos & Descriptions

Photos and symptom descriptions you submit are sent securely to our server solely to generate an AI health assessment. They are not stored on our servers after the analysis is complete, and are never shared with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

👤

Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data required to operate the app:

  • Photos and text descriptions you provide for analysis
  • Animal type selection (Dog, Cat, or Bird)
  • An anonymous device identifier (randomly generated UUID) used solely for daily usage rate-limiting — it cannot be used to identify you personally

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other personally identifiable information.

🕐 Data Retention

We retain data for the shortest time necessary:

  • Photos & descriptions: processed in real time and immediately discarded — not stored on any server
  • Anonymous device ID: retained in server memory for up to 24 hours for rate-limiting purposes, then automatically purged
  • App preferences (e.g. disclaimer acknowledged): stored locally on your device only, never transmitted to our servers

🗑️ How to Delete Your Data

Because we do not store photos or personal information on our servers, there is no account or user profile to delete.

To remove all locally stored app data (preferences, anonymous device ID) from your device:

  • Go to your device Settings → Apps → VetAIHealth → Storage → Clear Data
  • Or simply uninstall the app — this removes all local data immediately

If you have any data-related requests, contact us at: infovetaihealth@gmail.com

🛡️

Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and our server is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Our backend is hosted on Google Cloud Run and uses Google's Gemini AI API for analysis. We do not sell, rent, or trade your data.

🔗

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob – displays rewarded ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalisation subject to your consent choices.
  • Google Gemini API – processes your photo/text to generate the health assessment.
  • Google Cloud Run – hosts our backend server.
⚠️ Important Notice

VetAIHealth provides AI-generated preliminary assessments for informational purposes only. It does not replace the examination or opinion of a licensed veterinarian. Always consult a qualified veterinarian for diagnosis and treatment.
